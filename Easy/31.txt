Ben is very broke. 
He cannot afford rent, so he is going to get a job this month so he won't be evicted. 
The job he is going to get pays P per hour, and he works H hours, 
and his rent costs R each month. 
Output YES if Ben can afford his rent with extra money, 
NO if he cannot afford his rent, 
and BARELY if he can afford his rent with no extra money 

Input 
Line 1: an integer P - the amount Ben earns each hour he works 
Line 2: an integer H - the number of hours Ben works per month 
Line 3: an integer R - the monthly rent Ben must pay 

Output 
Line 1: YES if Ben can afford his rent with extra money, 
NO if he cannot afford his rent, 
and BARELY if he can afford his rent with no extra money 

Constraints 
0<P, R, H<1000 

Example 
Input 
20 
100 
600 

Output 
YES




-------Only see the solution after putting your efforts into it---------

//reading input
const P = parseInt(readline());
const H = parseInt(readline());
const R = parseInt(readline());

const earnings = P * H;

if(earnings > R) console.log("YES");
else if(earnings === R) console.log("BARELY");
else console.log("No");
