Sum of natural numbers from a to b inclusively

Input
Line 1 : Two space-separated integers a and b

Output
Line1 : Sum of natural numbers from a to b

Constraints
0 < a < b < 1000000

Example
Input
5 6

Output
11




-------Only see the solution after putting your efforts into it---------

//read inputs
const [a,b] = readline().split(" ").map(Number);
let sum= 0;
for(let i=a;i<=b;i++){
    sum+=i;
}

console.log(sum);
